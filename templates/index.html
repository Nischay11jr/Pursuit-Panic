Pursuit Panic

You play as a fearless street racer who just pulled off a daring heist. As you speed away from the scene, you realize the cops are hot on your tail, and you need to escape before they catch you.

PLAY

Instructions

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the car's movement.
  • Press the up arrow key to move the car forward.
  • Press the left and right arrow keys to move the car left and right, respectively.
  • Press the down arrow key to move it backwards.
  • Dodge the obstacles on the road by moving left or right to avoid them.
  • The obstacles will appear randomly and move in different directions, so be prepared to move in any direction to avoid them.
  • Collect coins on the road to increase your score.
  • If the police car catches up to you, the game is over.
  • The longer you stay ahead of the police car and the more coins you collect, the higher your score will be.
